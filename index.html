    Introduction¶

    Injectify is a web based MiTM tool, similiar to BeEF (although completely unrelated in terms of source code).

    What can it do?¶

    • Create a reverse Javascript shell between the victim and the attacker.
    • Records keystrokes and logs them to a database.
    • Extract and log saved passwords from the browser.
